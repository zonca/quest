Claudia Cencelli

Realtor® | San Diego • La Jolla • Coastal Properties

With more than 20 years of experience as a San Diego real estate agent, Claudia Cencelli is known for her deep expertise, caring approach, and unwavering commitment to her clients. Specializing in Coastal properties, La Jolla real estate, and the greater San Diego market, Claudia brings a rare blend of knowledge, intuition, and heart to every transaction.

Claudia holds advanced expertise in Trust and Probate real estate, National and International Relocation, and Guided Senior Living Transition, supporting clients through some of life’s most important real estate transitions with confidence, clarity, and compassion. Her philosophy, Real Estate Real Simple, reflects her belief that buying or selling a home should feel seamless, supported, and stress-free.

Clients describe Claudia as personable, reliable, and exceptionally knowledgeable. Always available, she listens deeply, communicates clearly, and is known for delivering a “knowledge with a heart” experience.

Whether you're buying, selling, relocating, or navigating a complex real estate situation in San Diego, La Jolla, or the coastal communities, Claudia brings professionalism, warmth, and real-world expertise to help you achieve your goals—simply and successfully.
